WHERE WE STARTED

Sustainable Scribbles had its humble beginnings in the Entrepreneurship 12 class, where our small team embarked on a mission to develop a sustainable and eco-friendly product. With the guidance of our teacher, Mrs. Stanyer, we passionately crafted our brand, determined to make a positive impact on the environment. our journey began with a shared commitment to creating a product that not only sparks creativity but also contributes to a greener planet.

OUR GOAL

As a brand, our primary goal is to offer eco-friendly and sustainable alternatives to traditional pencil crayons, contributing to a healthier environment. By prioritizing environmentally conscious practices, we aim to fulfill the objectives of UN Sustainable Goal #12, "fostering responsible consumption and production" and UN Sustainable Goal #11, "striving to make cities and human settlements more sustainable". Through our products, we aspire to promote creativity while leaving a positive impact on both local and global scales.

WHERE WE ARE

As a brand, Sustainable Scribbles has reached a significant milestone in its journey. Currently engaged in continuous manufacturing, the team is witnessing steady growth each day. After months of unwavering dedication and hard work, we are delighted to announce that our eco-friendly and sustainable product is ready to hit the market. This marks a pivotal moment as we strive to make a positive impact on the environment through our offerings.

WHERE WE'RE GOING

As a brand committed to sustainability, Sustainable Scribbles envisions a future where everyone effortlessly incorporates eco-friendly choices into their daily lives, making a positive impact on the planet while expressing their creativity. We plan to expand our range of sustainable products and make them accessible around the globe! Join us on this journey, where creativity meets responsibility, and explore the endless potential for a greener and more sustainable tomorrow.
